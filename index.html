About the Research

Specializing at the intersection of systems biology and digital clinical intervention, my work focuses on deciphering the complex multi-scale architectures of neurovascular disorders.

The Ischemic Emergency Protocol represents a milestone in my computational journey integrating real-time spatial genomics with predictive hemodynamics to map the Cascade Atlas of the Neurovascular Unit (NVU).
